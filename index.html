Long ago, the four nations lived together in harmony. But then, everything changed when the fire nation attacked...

WATER

Water is the element of change. The people of the Water Tribe are capable of adapting to many things.

EARTH

Earth is the element of substance. The people of the Earth Kingdom are diverse and strong. They are persistent and enduring.

FIRE

Fire is the element of power. The people of the fire nation have the desire, will, energy, and drive to achieve what they want.

AIR

Air is the element of freedom. The people of the Air Nomads detach themselves from worldly concerns and found peace and freedom.

Aang is the last surviving Airbender. He is an incarnation of the "Avatar", the spirit of light and peace manifested in human form. As the Avatar, Aang controls all four elements and is tasked with keeping the Four Nations at peace. With the world at war, he must defeat the Fire Lord to restore peace. However, he must first master all four elements. Luckily, he and his friends embark an an emir journey around the world to do so.

Katara is smart, capable, caring, kind, brave, passionate, and sometimes stubborn. She grew up in the Southern Water tribe and joins Aang on his quest to master the four elements and defeat the Fire Lord. Being from the Southern Water Tribe, she is a water bender, and a gifted one at that. Just like Aang, she has experienced loss. As a child, her mother was killed by raiders, forcing her to assume responsibility and acting more motherlike.

Sokka is smart, calculating, witty, inventive, and a natural leader. Sokka is the older brother of Katara. Unlike his sister, he does not have waster bending. Instead, he fights with more traditional combat. His weapons are his boomerang, his sward, but most importantly, his mind. He is the leader of Team Avarat and his plans effectively carry the team.

Toph is an extremely talented and masterful earthbender. Toph has been blind since birth, but due to her extensive earthbending skills, she can locate objects and their movements by sensing their vibrations in the earth around her. She leaves her home to join Aang on his quest and teaches him earth bending.

Zuko is the prince of the fire nation, and son of the Fire Lord. As a young teen, he was banished and could not return home unless he captures the avatar. Zuko spends the next few years hunting Aang down. Eventually, he has regained his honor and is welcomed home, but after traveling around the world, he realizes how evil his father is. He abandons his home and joins Aang and teaches him fire bending.
