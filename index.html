Long ago, the four nations lived together in harmony. But then, everything changed when the fire nation attacked...

WATER

Water is the element of change. The people of the Water Tribe are capable of adapting to many things.

EARTH

Earth is the element of substance. The people of the Earth Kingdom are diverse and strong. They are persistent and enduring.

FIRE

Fire is the element of power. The people of the fire nation have the desire, will, energy, and drive to achieve what they want.

AIR

Air is the element of freedom. The people of the Air Nomads detach themselves from worldly concerns and found peace and freedom.
